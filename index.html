Map

This map shows the location of COVID-19 cases in Missouri as a rate of cases per 100,000 people, as opposed to raw case numbers which would show dark spots in Kanas City and St. Louis, where there are more people generally.

Cases are normalized using 2018 Census population estimates. Kansas City cases are reflected in Jackson County and Joplin cases are reflected in Jasper County.





Daily case and death change

This chart shows the number of new cases reported by the Department of Health and Senior Services each day. Case totals will increase even if the positivity rate does not go up. The number of new cases on April 24 jumped due to a reporting error and does not reflect increases within the previous 48 hours.
